Meeting Viewer

 

A Meeting Viewer is used to run a meeting. The Meeting Viewer panel allows you to edit the viewer’s properties.

 

Logo

 

The Logo is displayed in the upper left corner of the viewer. You can upload your own logo. Note that if your logo has a different aspect ratio than the display area, it will be stretched to fit the display area.

 

Background

 

The Background is displayed in the main window area of the viewer when a meeting is not in progress or when there is no content being displayed. You can choose from a list of pre-defined background pictures.

 

Attendee

 

You can control how the attendee area of the meeting viewer is displayed. You can allow attendees to see everyone’s name, or only the moderator’s name. You can also allow an attendee to only send messages to the moderator.

 

Alert Sound

 

Elect to play sound when attendee joins or message arrives.

 

Exit Page

 

You can redirect attendees to any page when a meeting ends.

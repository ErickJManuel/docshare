Share Library Content

 

Clicking the Share library content icon opens a menu to access My Library and view the content. The content in both “Public Library” and “My Library” will be displayed in a drop-down menu. You can click on “Browser Libraries” to open a separate window to browse or upload library content. You can also import a PowerPoint presentation on-the-fly by selecting “Import Presentation”. Select “Refresh Menu” to update the menu after you have uploaded new content.

 

Select an item in the drop-down menu to open it. In the following example, we have already uploaded a PowerPoint presentation, Technical Training Series Intro.ppt. Clicking on the file name will share the first slide of the presentation with all participants. You can also share a video or audio file from the library in the same way.
